SassyCron


This cron job runs every hour and asks the question: How many alerts within the past 24 hours (from now), with a real/bogus score > 0.5, have a host in the GLADE catalog within the circumscribed circle of the ZTF cutout FoV (45″) at a distance of < 500 Mpc that are not in the TNS database and are not known solar system objects? For each such candidate, we then query the Alerce database to obtain a classifier and produce images of the cutouts. The table is explorable using the standard SASSy controls on the left.
